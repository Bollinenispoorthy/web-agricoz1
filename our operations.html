Our Foundational Agricultural Service Areas

Our operations at **Agricoz** are built on a foundation of deep industry knowledge, cutting-edge technology, and a relentless pursuit of client success. Each service area is led by seasoned professionals committed to delivering tailored, high-impact solutions.

1. Tissue Culture Lab – Advancing Agri-Biotechnology

Our cutting-edge **tissue culture laboratory** plays a pivotal role in producing **disease-resistant, high-yielding, and climate-adaptive plant varieties**. By using **micropropagation techniques**, we ensure consistent quality, uniformity, and faster plant growth, reducing dependency on traditional propagation methods.

  • High-yield, disease-free plantlets
  • Faster growth cycles with enhanced resistance
  • Precision-driven quality control and plant health management

Nourishing the Soil Naturally

3. Bio-Organic Manure & Agri-Inputs Production

Sustainability is at the heart of **Agricoz**. We **produce and distribute premium-quality bio-organic manure** to enrich soil fertility and enhance crop productivity without harmful chemicals. Our **organic fertilizers and bio-compost solutions** are designed to **rejuvenate soil health, improve microbial activity, and enhance water retention capacity**.

  • 100% organic, eco-friendly manure
  • Rich in essential nutrients for sustainable farming
  • Improves soil structure and promotes long-term productivity

These organic fertilizers are supplied to **farmers, agri-enterprises, and export markets**, ensuring a shift towards sustainable farming solutions.

4. Post-Harvest Processing & Agri-Product Distribution

We don't just grow—we innovate at every step. Our **post-harvest processing units** ensure that fresh produce is **graded, sorted, packed, and processed** under stringent quality control. This allows us to maintain **freshness, increase shelf-life, and reduce food wastage** before shipping to **domestic and international markets**.

Processed & Packaged Products Include:

  • Organic dried fruits & vegetables
  • Fresh-cut, ready-to-use produce
  • Natural fruit concentrates and pulps

At **Agricoz**, we believe in creating a **harmonious ecosystem where agriculture, forestry, and community welfare coexist**. Our **forest community development initiative** focuses on:

  • **Reforestation & Afforestation** – Planting trees and restoring green cover for ecological balance.

Our Commitment to Sustainability & Ethical Farming

At **Agricoz**, we uphold **organic, sustainable, and regenerative agriculture** as core principles. We are dedicated to:

  • **Zero Waste Farming** – Recycling organic matter back into the soil.
  • **Chemical-Free Cultivation** – Avoiding synthetic pesticides and fertilizers.
  • **Water Conservation** – Implementing drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting.
  • **Empowering Farmers** – Supporting rural communities with training and fair trade practices.
